Miscellaneous Info

Armada

The Armada system in Honkai Impact 3rd is basically a clan

Commissions Tab: Submit various materials for rewards

Warehouse Tab: Request and receive various materials

Contributions Tab: Shows contributions from daily and weekly missions by different Armada members

Armada Shop Tab: Shop that has various items to purchase with Ranger Creds received from Armada activites

Sim Battle is a gamemode where you fight 3 individual bosses for rewards and it's available twice a week

Dorm

The dorm system is a feature where you can interact with various character chibi forms and also customize the dorm with furniture

Example of a chibi form:

Dorm bonuses give Stamina for gathering resources + completing story stages and coins for various upgrades

The Dorm Shop offers various items you can purchase by using Work Points gotten from Errands

Expeditions offers character fragments and other materials

Divine Keys are weapons that offer special skills (basically universal buffs)

The Dorm Squad feature gives stat bonuses to certain battlesuits and upgrading the Dorm Squad improves dorm functions and stat bonuses

AstralOP

Astral Ops (Astral Operators) are characters that assist you in battle when a playable character enters Stellar Outburst (Battle feature only certain playable characters have)

ELFS

ELFS stand for Equipment: Living Form and they also help your characters in battle with their Basic Attack, Ultimate, Passive, and Team skills

Gacha/Supply Info

Honkai Impact 3rd's gacha banner system is called Supplies

Types of Supply banners:

Battlesuit Supply: Banner for limited S-rank (5-Star) characters

Equipment Supply: Banner for limted S-Rank stigmata and weapons

AstralOp Supply: Banner for limited S-Rank AstralOps

Event Supply: Banners for limited S-Rank Characters and limited Part 2 S-Rank Characters

Outfit Supply: Banner for limited 5-star outfits

Dorm Supply: Banner for all B, A, and old S-Rank Characters

Starter Supply: Banner for newer players/accounts

ELF Supply: Banner for ELFs

LTO (Limted Time Offers)

The LTO Shop is basically the place to spend currencies bought by microtransactions to buy deals

Hot section: Hot shop deals

Bundles section: Has Event Special, Recommended, Permanent, and Cyclic (Weekly) deals

Top Up section: Has microtransactions and a tab for bonus rewards that give certain items for amount of money spent

Shop

The regular shop has 4 different tabs

Daily Shop: Has 5 different sections that either reset daily, weekly, or resets at the beginning of a new version patch

Activity Shop: 6 Sections for items that can be bought from certain gamemode/feature currency points

Basic Currency: 2 sections for items that can be bought with basic currency (Gold and Asterite)

Event Shop: Various sections for items that can be bought from earning event points

Bonuses

There's a daily sign-in every month and all the items are the same expect the character fragment changes every month

Share Invitation is basically a referal code that new players can use

Monthly Card Bonus is a $4.99 USD microtransaction that gives additional crystals daily and you get 500 extra crystals every 15 days

Battle Pass

Honkai Impact 3rd's Battle Pass is 100 tiers (+50 extra tiers if you complete it early)and you can progress through it by completing daily and weekly missions

The BP (Battle Pass) Shop has various items that can be bought from getting BP store currency from the Battle Pass

You can upgrade your free BP (Vanguard BP) to Knight BP ($9.99 USD) or Paladin BP ($19.99 USD) for extra rewards
