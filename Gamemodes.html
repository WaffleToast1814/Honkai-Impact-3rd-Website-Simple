Gamemodes

The recommended gamemodes tab gives recommendations on what gamemodes to play

Top left tab: Directs you to the open world screen with 4 different options

Two out of the four of the open worlds are called A Post Honkai Odyssey Part 1 and Part 2 (APHO 1 and APHO 2)

The playable characters Adam and Timido are only playable in APHO

Top middle tab: Directs you to the main story tab

Top right tab: Directs you to the Realms of Battle gamemode

Bottom left tab: Directs you to Voyage Log

Bottom right tab: Directs you to Material Expedition

Q-Manifold or Superstring Dimension: Honkai Impact 3rd's Abyss gamemode

Memorial Arena: Gamemode to fight bosses

Universal Mirage: Gamemode to farm materials for craftable equipment

Elysian Realm: Gamemode that's the predecessor to Honkai Star Rail's Simulated Universe

Character Trials: Tab directing you to various character trial stages

Deep Archives: Tab directing you to the Herrscher Files and Aesir Files

Tactics Class: Tutorial stages for various game mechanics

Trials: Tab directing you to character trials

Tabs on the right: Version long events
